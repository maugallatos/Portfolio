T1

T1 Pages is a CRM owned by the Carso Group. The platform is divided into four components: T1 Envios , focusing on shipping methods, warehouses, etc.; T1 Pagos, focusing on payment methods; T1 Comercios, aimed at positioning sellers' products on marketplaces such as Sear, Sanborns, and Claroshop (all part of the Carso Group); and finally, T1 Páginas , a platform where anyone can build their e-commerce and also features a POS platform for physical retail.

My role within the company is primarily focused on T1 Pages, where I work as a web designer and UX/UI designer. I am part of the team that works on the platform's design, improving the sellers' experience within it, and also contributing to the construction of templates so that sellers have a foundation to quickly and easily build their pages. Additionally, I often participate as part of the Quality Assurance team, helping to test the developments carried out by the rest of the team.
